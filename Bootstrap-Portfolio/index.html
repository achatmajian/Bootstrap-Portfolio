About Me

I’m a UX Designer and Web Developer based in Brooklyn, NY. I’m a problem solver who builds websites that are intuitive and delightful for users by applying an empathic and user-centric approach in my creative process. From low fidelity wireframes, high fidelity mock-ups, to development I help my clients by taking their ideas from conception to execution.

When I’m not working I’m usually checking out an art show or exploring a museums new exhibit. As hobbies I’m passionate about painting, photography, cooking, and riding my bike. I’d love to hear about your next project!
